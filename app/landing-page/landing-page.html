Cryptocurrency Mining Calculator

A free browser-based tool providing predictive block-solving statistics and cost projection for cryptocurrency mining.

Math & logic written by a quantum astro physicist. Seriously.
UI by some dude with a Mac and a Bootstrap release.

Seems cool?

We've got two things going on here, a tool allowing you to forecast miner performance based on custom variables and another allowing you to check the performance of your current miner by providing an Ethereum address.

At this stage we're mainly focused on Ethereum, however you'll find you can apply aspects of the forecasting tool to most cryptocurrencies.

Statistical Forecasting

Provides block-solving and cost forecasting and statsitics for a miner given a set of input variables (eg, hashrate, difficulty, power costs etc).

Provides optional difficulty prediction using real-time blockchain statistics and cool math.

Launch Statistical Forecaster

Miner Performance Analysis

Enter your miners Ethereum address and see how it is performing compared to its statistical potential.

This is very useful for detecting issues in your miner that are directly affecting your profits.

Launch Miner Analysis

Compatibility & Privacy

We used Chrome to develop this. You can probably use another broswer but testing for cross-compatibility is boring and we didn't do it. If you're running a up-to-date browser (that isn't IE) and you have an issue, raise it on our GitHub.

We don't collect any data, we promise. You can even check our open-source code.

We do use some external, public APIs to provide us with data. They may or may not be logging requests, but the data they receive is so insignificant you don't need to worry about it unless you're into some really shady stuff.
